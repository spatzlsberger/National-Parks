Attendance at Yellowstone National Park through the Years

Since it’s establishment in 1872, Yellowstone National Park has attracted millions of visitors to view its expansive wildlife, breathtaking geysers, and hike its over 900 miles of trails. Several key moments have shaped Yellowstone in the last 30 years. Those moment’s impact can be seen in part in the change of annual visitors each year.
